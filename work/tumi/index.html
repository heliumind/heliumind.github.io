I am a Volunteer and Board member at ESN TUMi München. TUMi is part of the Erasmus Student Network, a Europe-wide student organization with the mission to foster student mobility in higher education. At TUMi we facilitate the social and personal integration of international students by providing support at the beginning of their studies studies in Munich and offering a diverse cultural and orientation program during the semester. Through my role as TUMi’s Local Representative I actively engage in ESN’s large network both on a national and international level.
